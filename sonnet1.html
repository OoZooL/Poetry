My first (might also be my last) sonnet / DaVe


It is such I cannot tell the difference

between daisies and daffodil, nor how

the nocturnal reverie would give 'ssence

to the singing of the nightingale, wow.

Everybody can pinpoint exactly their first

an occurrence of a certain one thing

but who can ever know to quench the thirst

and determine the last of each happ’ning.

One instance can be missed, other 'verdone,

the present is liquid, the past was solid,

the future unknown for most of ev'ryone,

the fluidity of time is mark’d on grid.

For the first of its kind, it is not bad,

This prattle bores me, I wish I were dead…
